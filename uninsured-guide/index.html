Healthcare platforms for the uninsured

A cash-pay guide · Prices verified 2026-07-03 – 2026-07-07

No insurance doesn’t mean sticker price. These platforms sell prescriptions, telehealth, and primary care for cash — often a fraction of retail. Pick one or combine. 0penRX is independent and takes no commission; figures are vendors’ own prices (see Sources) and change often. For a deeper dive, see the Amazon vs Costco vs GoodRx vs Walmart comparison.

Start with your situation

No single platform covers everything. Pick what you need — each routes to the verified options below.
