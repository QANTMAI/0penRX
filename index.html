Coupon guide

The universal GoodRx-network discount card below is the one pharmacy coupon any cash-paying or uninsured person can use as-is. Manufacturer copay/assistance programs (which require commercial insurance or per-patient enrollment) aren't cash coupons — they're linked on each drug's detail page instead. Verify any program before use; never use alongside insurance.

⚠️ Cash-pay only. Do not submit to insurance. Cannot be used with Medicare, Medicaid, TRICARE, VA, or any government health program. If you have insurance, verify your copay first — it may be lower. Out-of-pocket costs from these programs do not count toward Medicare Part D TrOOP.
