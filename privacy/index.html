Privacy

Last updated 2026-07-03

0penRX is built to know as little about you as technically possible. The short version: we collect no personal information, use no cookies, run no analytics or third-party trackers, have no accounts, and never sell or share anything.

What we collect

Nothing personal. There is no sign-up, no login, and no form that asks for who you are. We set no advertising or analytics cookies. The only thing stored in your browser is your light/dark theme choice (orx-theme in local storage) — it never leaves your device.

No third-party trackers

0penRX loads no analytics scripts, no advertising pixels, no social widgets, and no third-party fonts. Fonts are served from our own domain. A strict Content-Security-Policy blocks any script or connection we did not explicitly allow.

Live drug data

When you open a drug, your browser queries public government/FDA services (NLM RxNorm, openFDA, CMS NADAC) directly, and — for assistance programs — our own read-only backend. Those requests carry the drug name you looked up and standard network metadata (like your IP address), handled by each service under its own policy. We do not log your searches, and we keep drug names and search terms out of our own logs and analytics by design. We store none of it.

No accounts, no selling, no sharing

There are no user accounts and no personal data to sell, share, or lose. Because we collect nothing, there is nothing to request, export, or delete — and no "Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information" mechanism is needed.

Reporting a concern

Security or privacy issue? See /.well-known/security.txt or our security policy ↗.

0penRX is an independent information service and provides reference information only — not medical, legal, insurance, or pricing advice. Verify prices and coupons at the pharmacy before use.
